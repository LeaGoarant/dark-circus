Who are we ? You may ask. We are a family, we are performers, we are lovers, we are travellers. We are weird, and misshapen. We are nature's error, but still marvelous. You are invited to join us in our journey, to discover our world of oddities, for a night, or a day. Don't get scared, because we just want to amaze you !
